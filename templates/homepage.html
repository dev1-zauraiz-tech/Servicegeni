What is ServiceGeni?

ServiceGeni is your all-in-one platform designed to supercharge automotive shop performance. By combining smart customer engagement tools with powerful analytics, we deliver everything you need to grow your business.

Our engagement suite includes integrated VoIP phones, SMS messaging, call recording with intelligent analysis, and digital vehicle inspections to keep your customers informed and confident.

Meanwhile, our real-time analytics dashboard tracks advisor and technician performance, monitors shop goals, and provides crucial financial insights through innovative features like Outrunning Overhead and P&L tracking. With ServiceGeni, you'll have the tools to deliver outstanding service while driving sustainable growth.
